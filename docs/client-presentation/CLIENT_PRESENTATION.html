Complete Patient Workflow

üìãPhase 1: Patient Entry & Registration

New Patient Process:

  • Receptionist navigates to Patient Registration
  • Fill comprehensive form: Personal details, medical history, allergies, emergency contacts
  • System auto-generates unique Patient ID
  • Next Step: Add to queue or schedule appointment

Returning Patient Process:

  • Search by name, phone, or patient ID
  • Verify identity and update information if needed
  • Review medical alerts and previous history

üìÖPhase 2: Appointment & Queue Management

  • Schedule Consultation: Select patient ‚Üí Choose doctor ‚Üí Pick time slot ‚Üí Add chief complaint
  • Queue Entry: Patient automatically added to today's consultation queue
  • Status Flow: waiting ‚Üí confirmed ‚Üí arrived ‚Üí ready for consultation

üë®‚Äç‚öïÔ∏èPhase 3: Doctor Consultation & Diagnosis

  • Clinical Assessment: Record chief complaint, examination findings
  • üéØ CRITICAL: Establish definitive diagnosis (root cause identification)
  • Treatment Planning: Create comprehensive treatment plan
  • Decision Matrix: Choose treatment path based on diagnosis

üíäPhase 4: Treatment Decision & Routing

Treatment Options:

  • üè• Procedures Required: Custom quotes ‚Üí Admin review ‚Üí Procedures department
  • üíä Medicines Only: Prescription notes ‚Üí Direct to pharmacy
  • üîÑ Both Required: Procedures first ‚Üí Pharmacy ‚Üí Billing
  • ‚úÖ No Treatment: Direct to billing (consultation only)

üë®‚ÄçüíºPhase 5: Admin Review & Pricing Approval

  • Review Process: Examine doctor's quotes and medical reasoning
  • Pricing Factors: Patient condition, insurance coverage, hospital policies
  • Actions: Approve/reject individual procedures
  • Routing: Approved ‚Üí Procedures, Rejected ‚Üí Pharmacy or Billing

üè•Phase 6: Treatment Execution

  • Procedures: Execute approved treatments ‚Üí Mark complete ‚Üí Route to pharmacy/billing
  • Pharmacy: Dispense medicines ‚Üí Patient counseling ‚Üí Mark complete ‚Üí Route to billing

üí≥Phase 7: Final Billing & Payment

  • Bill Generation: Consultation + Approved procedures + Dispensed medicines
  • Calculations: Subtotal + Taxes + Discounts = Total amount
  • Payment: Multiple methods (cash, card, UPI, insurance, bank transfer)
  • Invoice: Generate professional invoice and receipt
